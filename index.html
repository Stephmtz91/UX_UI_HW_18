    About Me

    Hi! I'm Stephanie Martinez and I'm a passionate UX researcher.I am dedicated to understanding the needs and behaviors of users to create exceptional digital experiences. With a background in psychology and a love for technology, I bring a unique perspective to my work. My journey as a UX researcher began with my fascination for human behavior and how it intersects with technology. I believe that by truly understanding users, we can design products that not only meet their needs but also delight them.

    My UX Skills

    Research

    Conducting interviews, surveys, and observation to gather valuable insights, enabling the creation of user centered products and experiences. 

    Prototying

    Using various tools to create lo-fi and hi-fi prototypes, allowing for iterative testing, refinement, and validation design concepts

    Information Architecture

    Exploring users needs using methods such as: card sorting, sitemaps, and wire frames to ensure findability, clarity, and ease of use for users.
